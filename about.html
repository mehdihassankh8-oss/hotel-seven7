About this property

Comfortable Accommodations: Hotel Seven 7 offers family rooms with private bathrooms, air-conditioning, and city views. Each room includes a balcony, refrigerator, and free toiletries.

Exceptional Facilities: Relax in the garden or on the terrace. Free WiFi throughout. Hot tub, lounge, and outdoor seating available.

Dining Options: Breakfast includes continental, vegetarian, halal, gluten-free, and Asian. On-site restaurant serves a variety of cuisines.

Convenient Location: Located 12 mi from Jinnah International Airport, near Teen Talwar. Free private parking. Highly rated for staff and location.
